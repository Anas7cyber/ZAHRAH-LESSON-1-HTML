Artemis Fowl

Artemis Fowl, a twelve‑year‑old criminal genius, kidnaps fairy officer Holly Short to ransom fairy gold but discovers the are dangerous and high‑tech. He later allies with them to rescue his father, recovers a stolen supercomputer, and thwarts a plot to destroy the fairy people. Adventures escalate into missions involving a parallel fairy colony, a time‑travel attempt to save his mother, and a battle with an ancient Atlantean threat that fractures his mind. In the finale, Artemis and his allies race to stop an apocalyptic plan, forcing sacrifices and testing loyalties.
